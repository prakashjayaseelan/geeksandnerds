Geeks and Nerds

Welcome to Geeks and Nerds, it’s a brand new Co- working space started in the year 2022. Located at Banashankari 2nd Stage of Bangalore just 25 meters from Banashankari metro station. Founded by IT Professional couple with an agenda to reinvent conventional working environment to innovative Co-workspace for start-ups, Entrepreneurs and well-established organisations. We focus on providing impeccable Workspace, with an emphasis on Ergonomically designed workstations, Well trained staff for your conveniences with best amenities


We hope you enjoy our Services as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don’t hesitate to contact us.

Our Vision

Our future vision is to expand our business model with active innovations to collaborate among various other clients across the country.

Our Mission

“Geeks and Nerds, connect your passion” is evolving the way people work. By seamlessly providing quality work ambience for Freelancers, Entrepreneurs and Independent professionals and Start-ups to achieve their passion through our space. Our Mission is to revolutionize traditional work system to more convenient and accessible work location.
